# Cohort analysis that reallocated $1.2M of outreach spend

**Situation:** Sheba Commerce was spreading a $3.1M annual outreach budget evenly across
channels on gut feel, with no view of which channels actually retained customers.

**Task:** Determine where the outreach budget earned its keep and make a defensible case to
finance for moving it.

**Action:** Built customer cohorts by acquisition channel in SQL/Python, tracked 90-day
retention and contribution margin per cohort, and controlled for seasonality. Presented a
ranked channel-ROI view and a reallocation proposal to the CMO and finance.

**Result:** Finance reallocated **$1.2M** from two break-even channels into three channels
running ~3x ROI; blended retained-customer CAC improved 27% the following two quarters.
