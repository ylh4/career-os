# Built a self-serve BI practice from zero

**Situation:** Every metric question at Sheba Commerce became a ticket to the data team;
analysts spent half their week answering ad-hoc "what's the number?" requests.

**Task:** Move the org from ticket-driven reporting to trustworthy self-serve analytics.

**Action:** Defined a governed semantic layer (consistent metric definitions), built ~20
curated Looker dashboards for the top recurring questions, and ran fortnightly data-literacy
sessions so non-analysts could answer their own questions safely.

**Result:** Weekly active dashboard users grew from **0 to 180 in six months**; ad-hoc
data-request tickets fell by roughly half, freeing analysts for deeper work.
